I'm a musician born in Naples (Italy) but placed in Rome from years. My main instrument is the guitar but I play also bass, drum, keyboard and I'm always ready to learn to sing.

I started playing "smoke on the water" on my bed with an old classic guitar in the 2010, and after that I never stoped playing. I played in different bands in my carreer but only two are in my heart and are "Jokers" & "Handless".
My favorite artists are: Guns N'Roses, Nirvana, AC/DC, John Mayer, Twentyone Pilots, Pink Floyd, Aerosmith, Santana, Avenged Sevenfold, Metallica. So I'm a rock guy but I like play many kinds of music.
